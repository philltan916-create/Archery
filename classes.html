Archery Classes

Straight to the Point Archery offers several types of lessons to accommodate everyone from the first time archer to the professional archer.

A101 Basic Archery

A101 is a basic archery course that focuses on beginning archery safety, skills, and drills.

  • Cost: $120/month
  • Schedule: Mondays and Wednesdays, 4–5pm
  • Prerequisite: Minimum age 8 (or 7 with evaluation)

A102 Intermediate Archery

A102 focuses on intermediate archery safety and form while introducing new equipment styles such as recurve and compound bows.

  • Cost: $120/month
  • Schedule: Tuesdays and Thursdays, 4–5pm
  • Prerequisite: A101 or instructor recommendation

A103 Advanced Archery

This class focuses on tournament shooting techniques used by international archery organizations.

  • Cost: $120/month
  • Schedule: Tuesdays and Thursdays, 5–6pm
  • Prerequisite: A101/A102 or equivalent
